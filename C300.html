The 2015 C300 is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder engine that delivers 241 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. The 2015 Mercedes-Benz C300 provides decent fuel economy for a luxury sedan. The rear-wheel-drive version is rated at 25 mpg in the city and 34 mpg on the highway, while the all-wheel-drive version gets slightly lower numbers, with 24 mpg city and 31 mpg highway.
