Powering the 2024 Lexus IS 350 is a 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission in the rear-wheel-drive (RWD) model, while the all-wheel-drive (AWD) version comes with a 6-speed automatic. The IS 350 delivers strong acceleration and confident handling, with a 0-60 mph time of around 5.6 seconds.

FUEL ECONOMY: Despite its powerful V6 engine, the 2024 IS 350 maintains respectable fuel efficiency. The RWD version is rated at around 20 mpg in the city and 28 mpg on the highway, while the AWD model sees a slight dip to 19 mpg city and 26 mpg highway.

EXTERIOR DESIGN: The 2024 Lexus IS 350 boasts a bold, aggressive design that reflects its sporty nature.
